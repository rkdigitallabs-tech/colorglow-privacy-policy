Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Information We Collect

All Status Studio operates entirely offline. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All data remains on your device.

2. Storage Permissions

We request storage permissions to:

  • Access WhatsApp statuses from your device
  • Save media to your device's internal storage
  • Store edited content and favorites locally

3. Vault Security

Files stored in the Vault are encrypted using AES-256 encryption and remain on your device. We cannot access or recover your encrypted files if you forget your PIN.

4. Third-Party Services

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect data as per Google's privacy policy. We recommend reviewing Google's privacy policy for details.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or distribute any user data as we do not collect it in the first place.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the app.

7. Contact Us

For questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the app's feedback feature.
